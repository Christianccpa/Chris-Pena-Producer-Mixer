ABOUT

Chris is a talented producer, mixer, and composer from Medellín, Colombia, who specializes in heavy and rock music production. His expertise in music production has helped to shape the sound of many local artists and bands, such as Austin's Phobias, Sin and Grace, Living the panic, Herida de Guerra, Ocaso, among others. Chris has worked on countless songs, and his experience and skills have been a great contribution to the development of the local music scene. His abilities have allowed him to work across various genres and styles, and his focus on heavy and rock music has enabled him to develop a unique and distinctive style in his work.
